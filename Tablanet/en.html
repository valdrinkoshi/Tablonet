Rules

Tablanet is a turn-based card game. You can play only one card at each turn.
Your card can collect the table cards that match its number or sum to its number (e.g. your "5" can take all the "5", "2+3", and "4+1" on the table)
The number of A can be 1 or 11, J is 12, Q is 13, and K is 14.
Watch out with A: if there are 2 A on the table, you can take them either with J (11+1) or 2 (1+1)

Points

The player who has more points wins the match. Cards worth points are:
  • 10, A, J, Q, K = 1 point
  • 10♦ = 2 points
  • 2♣ = 1 point
  • other cards = 0 points
When you take all the cards on the table, you make Tablanet and you gain 1 extra point.
At the end of the game, the player who collected more cards gains 3 extra points.

How to play:

To take the cards from the table, first select them and then play your card.
You can also select the cards to take by holding one of your cards on top of them.
Undo your selection by tapping anywhere in the screen.

Enjoy!
